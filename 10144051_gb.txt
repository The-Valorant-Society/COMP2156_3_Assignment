George Brown College is a public college located in Toronto, Ontario, Canada.
The institution was established in 1967. Affiliated sports teams are branded as the Huskies with colours of blue and white.

George Brown College is not just about academics; it's a place where students find their passion and path to success. With a vibrant student life, cutting-edge research opportunities, and strong industry partnerships, George Brown stands out as a beacon of learning and innovation in Toronto.
